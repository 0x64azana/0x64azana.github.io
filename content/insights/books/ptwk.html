Book Report

Title

Penetration Testing with Kali LInux

Author

Offsec

Genre

Computing & Technology | Non-Fiction

Critique

This PDF is provided to those who have purchased the OSCP course from OffSec and serves as an introduction to penetration testing.

This was one of the first resources I read on my journey into cybersecurity. It effectively teaches both methodology and tools. OffSec released this PDF as a complete guide on how to pass the OSCP. Although penetration testing is not a linear process and cannot be mastered with a single PDF alone, this file does an excellent job introducing the various aspects needed during a pentest. Without the information contained in this PDF, I would never have been able to kickstart my career in cybersecurity.

As a source of knowledge, this PDF is undeniably a treasure. It is very easy to read and follow, with great pacing throughout. Overall, this is probably the resource I have referred to the most over time.

9/10
