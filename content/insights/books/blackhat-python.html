Book Report

Title

BlackHat Python 2nd Edition

Author

Justin Seitz

Genre

Computing & Technology | Non-Fiction

Critique

This opinion might be controversial, but I don’t think this book is a good read.

Although it covers many important concepts, the book is difficult not only to read but also to understand. In my view, you need to already be familiar with the material to get much out of it, as it does not explain concepts clearly or thoroughly enough. One might argue that “you simply need to do further research yourself,” but isn’t the point of a book to provide that foundational knowledge?

This book might serve well as a reference to keep on your shelf, but I did not find it enjoyable, and overall, I consider it to be of poor quality.

3/10
