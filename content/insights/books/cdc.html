Book Report

Title

Curl of the Dead Cow

Author

Joseph Menn

Genre

Computing & Technology | Non-Fiction

Critique

This boook is GREAT! It's been a while since I read it so the details are not quite there.

The book offers a fascinating and detailed look into the history of the hacker group "Cult of the Dead Cow." It explores their origins, key events, and the impact they’ve had on hacking culture and cybersecurity. From what I remember, many key members went on to become big names such as John McAfee. Through engaging storytelling, it sheds light on how this influential group shaped parts of the underground hacking scene.

Although I don’t remember every detail (It's been a few years since I read it), this book left a strong impression on me. It’s an enjoyable read that combines history with insight, making it a valuable addition for anyone interested in hacker culture and cybersecurity history.

8/10
