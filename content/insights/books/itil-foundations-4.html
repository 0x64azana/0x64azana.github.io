Book Report

Title

ITIL Foundation 4th Edition

Author

Axelos

Genre

IT Management

Critique

ITIL Foundation, 4th Edition serves as an introduction to Information Technology management, and it fulfills that purpose effectively.

As I read the book, it became clear how business and IT are interconnected, working together to create a smooth, efficient workflow. Although the book was easy to read, I found that its key concepts could essentially be summarized on a single page. Before the exam, I relied on that one-page summary graph to review and study. Compared to Project+, which focuses more on structured, regulated workflows, this book emphasizes fluid communication and how to move effectively from point A to point B.
