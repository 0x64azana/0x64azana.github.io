Book Report

Title

Red Team Field Manual v2

Author

Ben Clark & Nick Downer

Genre

Computing & Technology | Non-Fiction

Critique

As the name implies, this book is a 200-page reference sheet. It is not designed for teaching concepts from scratch but rather for applying knowledge effectively in the field.

This book is excellent. After reading it, I experienced a noticeable boost in confidence, gained a consolidated arsenal of tools and techniques, and reinforced my existing knowledge. The material is laid out in a very reader-friendly way, making it easy to find and apply information when needed.

Be advised, the book’s sole purpose is to serve as a practical reference guide for applicable knowledge. It’s an invaluable resource for professionals who want quick access to information without wading through extensive explanations.

8/10
