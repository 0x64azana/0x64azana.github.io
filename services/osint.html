What is this?

OSINT-Based Risk Profiling is the process of collecting and analyzing publicly available information about an individual or organization to assess potential security, reputational, and operational risks. This proactive approach helps you understand your digital footprint and address weaknesses before they can be exploited.

The Process:

Initial Consultation & Scope Definition:
We begin by discussing your objectives and defining the scope of the profile — whether it’s for an executive, employee, potential partner, or your entire organization.

Information Gathering:
Using Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) techniques, we collect information from public records, social media, breach databases, forums, news articles, and other legal, publicly accessible sources.

Analysis & Risk Assessment:
We analyze the collected data to identify potential risks — such as data leaks, sensitive personal information exposure, reputational red flags, fraudulent or conflicting activity, and security vulnerabilities.

Risk Scoring & Prioritization:
Each finding is assessed and scored based on severity, relevance, and potential impact. This helps prioritize which issues need immediate attention and which should be monitored over time.

Reporting:
You receive a clear, actionable report that outlines the findings, risk scores, and recommendations to reduce exposure and strengthen your online presence.

Presentation & Consultation:
We walk you through the report, answer any questions, and provide guidance on next steps — from removing sensitive data to improving privacy settings and policies.

Ongoing Monitoring (Optional):
Upon request, we can set up continuous monitoring to alert you of new risks, breaches, or changes to your digital footprint over time.
